About

Dr. Tahera Khaledi,

A passionate Health worker has done her graduation on, Bachelor of physiotherapy from Modern Institute of physical medicine & Rehabilitation, Dr.NTR university, Vijaywada in the year 2012.While pursuing her graduation she got fascinated by Chinese traditional treatment "Cupping Therapy". She learned and practiced " Cupping Therapy which is also known as Hijama from Unani Herbo medical association of India. She successfully treated many ailments with the same therapy. From the year 2012-2013 She worked as HOD at Mina Multi speciality hospital, Mehdipatnam & consulted at swathi Hospital, Banjara hills & Rebound physiotherapy clinic mehdipatnam.in the year 2017 She then explored her interest in Acupuncture and learned the Technique from "Rajasthan university".she worked Independently delivering health with physical therapy and Complimentary medicine. In the year 2018 she joined Health care at Home where she worked dedicatedly as consultant physiotherapist bagging many awards and Honour. She got a chance to do specialisation in knee pain accredited from CPD, UK where she successfully won the title of "Kneeologist" (Knee pain specialist). Making her First kneeologist from Hyderabad.To do more in the promoting health & her thirst to manage and reverse chronic diseases,she discovered that Food can act as medicine and hence she joined Advance medical Nutrition course & successfully completed it in 2020 from Lincoln University,Malaysia. With her intense knowledge & education she successfully treated & managed covid19 or Influenza like illness,obesity,Cardiovascular problem, Diabetes,PMS,PCOD,Thyroid & many other conditions with her diet. Being a physiotherapist & complimentary medicine expert she is delivering her knowledge and promoting health through Holistic approach.
